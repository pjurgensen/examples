#Common one-off database tasks:

* Create your test and development databases:
	$ rake db:create

* Drop your databases:
	$ rake db:drop

* Create an empty migration:
	$ rake db:new_migration

* Run a migration:
	$ rake db:migrate

* Roll back a migration:
	$ rake db:rollback

* Prepare your test database:
	$ rake db:test:prepare
